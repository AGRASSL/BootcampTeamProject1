Fridge Foodie

Turn 'What's Left' into Leftovers

Welcome!

We were inspired to build Fridge Foodie to help those who are food lovers, to get creative with the items they currently have on hand; while also bringing solace to those of us less confident in the kitchen.

According to the US Department of Agriculture, 30-40% of food in the US goes to waste. With millions having made the shift to remote work, there is undoubtedly an increase in at home meal creation. With that, we also hope Fridge Foodie helps you minimize food waste and maximize meal creativity.
